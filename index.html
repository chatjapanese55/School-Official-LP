A Word from Our Founder

Why I built this

Taiyo Iino

Founder & Head Instructor, Chat Japanese Tokyo

"I've seen too many people give up on Japanese because they were told it required years of expensive classes and endless hours at a desk. I knew there had to be a better way."

I traveled the world and saw how the "high wall" of language — high costs and long commitments — kept people from starting their Japanese journey. I wanted to change that. I believe that language shouldn't be a barrier; it should be a bridge to human connection.

That's why I started Chat Japanese: to lower those hurdles by focusing on real-life conversation. You don't need a fortune or years of study. Just show up with an open mind, and we'll help you start connecting with Tokyo from day one.
